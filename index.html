About Me

A native to Minnesota, Jordan Werre is an active piano performer, teacher, and accompanist. He graduated from the University of Minnesota with a B.A. in Music Theory and Mathematics, and served on the faculty at Saint Paul Conservatory for Performing Artists in Saint Paul, Minnesota as a Music Theory instructor. An award winning composer, his pieces have been performed throughout the Midwest.

Outside of music, Jordan enjoys golfing, hiking, bicycling, traveling and cooking. He lives in Clermont, Florida with his wife and daughter. They are very glad to be out of the Minnesota cold.

Jordan is excited to be part of the UCF Coding Bootcamp and looks forward to learning much, much more.
